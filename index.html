 You See a Girl, I See the Future.

When a girl is educated and empowered, she holds limitless potential for her future.

But in many places around the world, we aren't reaching girls- and the statistics are there to prove it. Currently, there are still 130 million girls out of school globally and for some, the promise of school is never quite a guarantee when the daily commute from home to school is nearly nine miles long. Over two thousands miles away, other girls face the scrutiny of society and it becomes clear why in 2017, only 19.4% of women are represented in U.S. Congress. Everyday, no matter where she is, a girl will have to endure hardship- simply for being a girl.

At the Her Future Project, we're tackling these issues by using the timeless power of artistic expression to empower women regionally, nationally, and internationally. Join us today by turning art into activism and invest in her future, one print at a time.
